# Data Dictionary

| S.No | Column Name | Data Type | Description | Sample Values |
|------|-------------|------------|-------------|---------------|
| 1 | Job Title | Categorical | Specifies the role or position offered in the job market. | Data Analyst, AI Engineer, Product Manager |
| 2 | Industry | Categorical | Represents the industry sector in which the organization operates. | Technology, Healthcare, Finance, Retail, Entertainment |
| 3 | Company Size | Categorical | Indicates the size of the organization. | Small, Medium, Large |
| 4 | Location | Categorical | Geographic location where the job opportunity is available. | Dubai, Singapore, Tokyo, San Francisco |
| 5 | AI Adoption Level | Categorical | Measures the extent of AI implementation within the organization. | Low, Medium, High |
| 6 | Automation Risk | Categorical | Indicates the likelihood of the role being affected by automation. | Low, Medium, High |
| 7 | Required Skills | Categorical | Key skills required to perform the job role. | Python, UX/UI Design, Marketing, Cybersecurity, Project Management |
| 8 | Salary USD | Numerical | Annual salary offered for the position in US Dollars. | 65000, 85000, 120000 |
| 9 | Remote Friendly | Categorical | Indicates whether the role supports remote work. | Yes, No |
| 10 | Job Growth Projection | Categorical | Represents the expected future demand for the role. | Growth, Decline |
