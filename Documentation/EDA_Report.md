# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Industry Distribution 
- Manufacturing has the highest number of AI-related job postong (58).
- Transportation has the lowest job count (39).

## Geographical Analysis
  -San Franciscp has the highest AI job opportunities.
  -Toronto has the lowest job count.
  
## Compensation analysis
-Finance offers the highest average salary.
-Transportation offers the lowest average salary.

## AI Adoption Analysis

- Higher AI adoption does not necessarily correspond to higher salaries.

## Workforce Flexibility Analysis

- Remote-friendly jobs have slightly higher average salaries than non-remote jobs.

## Skill Gap Analysis

- Python and Project Management are the most demanded skills.

## Automation Risk Analysis

- Automation risk is almost evenly distributed across jobs.

## Future Workforce Trends

- Job growth and decline projections are balanced.
